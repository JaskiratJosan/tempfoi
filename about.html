    Flavours of India is one of the famous Indian restaurants to open in Regina and is certainly among the best known, both for its great prices and great food. The cuisine is East Indian.

    About the restaurant

    Lorem

    Cooking is an art that comes straight from the heart, just like all passions in life - it takes a little effort, and a lot of inspiration. The joy cooking gives you comes from experimenting, transforming and ultimately creating something new.
    Flavours of India was established in 2006 thanks to the idea and the stamina of enterprising Indian couple, Sukhi and Balli Josan, who have been in Canada for many years.
    Located in the centre of Regina, FOI is the perfect place. You can either have a quick meal or a more formal dinner in an elegant yet comfortable ambience, where service is truly professional. In any case, it will be a little journey to India, to its flavours and colours.
    At Flavours of India you will find a wide selection of vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes.
